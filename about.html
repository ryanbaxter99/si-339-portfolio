About Me

Hello! My name is Tyler Baxter. I am a sophmore studying Psychology at Grand Valley State University. I love going on roadtrips, focusing on cinematography, hiking, watching anime, and creating content for my podcast. I consider myself to be both an understanding and ambitious person. I'm most ambitious about my podcast, The Common Phenomenon. This podcast has been my main hobby for months and I finally have everything needed to start recording and posting my podcasts. I started this podcast back in 2021 during the COVID-19 pandamic as a way to escape from the worlds endevors. As time progressed, the podcast became more than a hobbie and started to became a way to connect with manny different people from around the area and take a look into the lives of many unique individuals.

The Common phenomenon is a early in development project that I am very passionate about. I love connecting with the many unique individuals that I have had the opportunity to meet so far on my journey. I will continue to pour my blood, sweat and tears into this podcast as long as I possibly can. I hope you all enjoy your stay and again welcome to the Common Phenomenon.
